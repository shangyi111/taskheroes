Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

When you use TaskHeroes, we collect information you provide directly to us. This includes:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, password, and profile picture.
  • Profile Data: Information you choose to add to your profile, such as your location, skills, bio, and portfolio images.
  • Communications: Messages, job requests, and reviews you send to other users via our in-platform chat and review systems.
  • Verification Data: If you choose to undergo identity verification, you will provide sensitive documents directly to Stripe. We only collect the resulting "Verified" status provided back to us by Stripe.

2. How We Use Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the TaskHeroes platform.
  • Facilitate communication between Seekers and Providers.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our platform.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities to protect the rights and property of TaskHeroes and our users.

3. Information Sharing

TaskHeroes values your privacy. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: When you send a message, request a booking, or leave a review, your public profile information (such as your username and avatar) is shared with the interacting user.
  • With Service Providers: We share data with third-party vendors who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf, such as our email notification provider (Resend) and identity verification partner (Stripe).
  • For Legal Reasons: We may share information if we believe disclosure is in accordance with, or required by, any applicable law, regulation, or legal process.
